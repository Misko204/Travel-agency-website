 Berlin - the city that never sleeps

Berlin, the capital of Germany, is a metropolis that combines rich history with a modern spirit and cosmopolitan charm.

This city is known as a place where past and future meet at every corner – from historical monuments and museums to futuristic architecture and vibrant nightlife.

The most famous symbol of Berlin is the Brandenburg Gate, proudly standing as a witness to turbulent history and a symbol of German unity.

Remnants of the Berlin Wall remind visitors of the city’s divided past, while artistic installations at the East Side Gallery turn it into an open-air gallery.

The impressive Museum Island, protected by UNESCO, houses world-renowned art and archaeological collections.

At the heart of the city is the Reichstag, the German parliament building, whose glass dome offers a spectacular view over Berlin.

The city is also famous for its parks and green spaces, with the vast Tiergarten standing out in particular.

Berlin is a hub of culture, art, and music, with a rich program of concerts, festivals, and theater performances.

At the same time, the city exudes a modern rhythm through dynamic neighborhoods full of restaurants, cafés, and shops.

Whether you come for history, art, or entertainment, Berlin will provide an unforgettable experience and leave a strong impression on every visitor.
