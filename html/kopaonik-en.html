 Kopaonik - Serbian winter paradise

Kopaonik is the largest and most popular ski resort in Serbia, located in the central part of the country at an altitude of about 1,770 meters.

It is known for excellent skiing and snowboarding conditions, with over 60 km of well-maintained slopes of various difficulty levels.

Modern cable cars and ski lifts provide fast and comfortable access to all parts of the resort.

Besides winter sports, Kopaonik offers a rich après-ski program with restaurants, cafés, and nightclubs.

Special ski areas and schools for families and beginners make it suitable for all generations.

Kopaonik is also a national park, rich in flora and fauna, making it attractive in the summer for hiking and cycling.

Relaxation enthusiasts can enjoy wellness and spa centers located in numerous hotels and resorts.

Panoramic views of surrounding mountain peaks and wide slopes leave a special impression on every visitor.

Kopaonik is famous for sunny winter days, which is why it is called the “Mountain of the Sun.”

The combination of sport, entertainment, and natural beauty makes Kopaonik a perfect destination for an active and relaxing holiday all year round.
