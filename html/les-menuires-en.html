 Les Menuires - Paradise for Ski Lovers

Les Menuires is one of the most famous ski resorts in France, located in the heart of the Alps and part of the renowned “Three Valleys” ski area.

This modern winter resort is situated at 1,850 meters above sea level and offers excellent skiing conditions throughout the season.

With over 600 kilometers of interconnected slopes, Les Menuires is ideal for both beginners and experienced skiers and snowboarders.

The village is designed for easy access – from ski slopes to hotels, apartments, restaurants, and shops.

Besides skiing, visitors can enjoy activities like sledding, snowmobiling, ice skating, and mountain walks.

Les Menuires also has modern wellness centers and spa zones, perfect for relaxing after a day on the snow.

It is especially attractive for families, offering numerous activities and programs for children of all ages.

At night, the resort provides diverse entertainment through restaurants, bars, and apres-ski events, enhancing the holiday experience.

Stunning views of surrounding mountain peaks and the pure Alpine air leave a lasting impression on every visitor.

Les Menuires combines sport, fun, and relaxation, making it a perfect choice for a winter holiday.
